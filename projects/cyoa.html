CYOA: Escape Room

In order to further practice our coding and collaboration skills, my partners and I, Vivian and Siying Zheng, created a “Choose Your Own Adventure” repo which was escape room themed.

We then proceeded to make a google drawing mapping out our plans for this topic, it was eventually decided to do a horror based escape room for more flare and creativity. The player would wake up in an abandoned hospital and have to make a series of choices to avoid death. We used CS50 IDE in order to create our project and had three subdirectories for convenience. Then we created the plot and endings of our story, and then linked each file together.

One of my biggest takeaways from this project was that I properly learned how to link individual files together via CS50 IDE. I've already done it before, but this experience helped me fully grasp the concept. I can now confidently say that I know how to do something that previously confused me. The biggest challenge I experienced was collaborating with my partners, since we are in different classes. Thankfully we were able to communicate on Slack, but it was still difficult to get quick responses.
