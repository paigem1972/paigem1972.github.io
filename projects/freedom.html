The Future of Visual Arts

SEP10 Freedom Project

Context

The “Freedom Project,” for SEP10 is all about informing people on how technology can be incorporated into topics that normally don’t include it. The purpose of this blog is to show the process of creating a technological innovation that improves and further develops the future of visual art.

Content

For my project I made a website about visual arts using HTML, CSS, Tailwind CSS, and Github with my partner Vio Patt. To hypothesize on the future technology that can be created in visual arts we created VAV. This device will project a hologram of a 3D model that can be adjusted with the user's own hands. We hope that this device will serve as an art reference and make learning anatomy easier for beginner artists. Our website displays both our future ideas and past innovations along with context as to what visual arts is.

Takeaways:

One of my biggest challenges was the positioning of our website and collaborating with my partner. As Vio and I are in separate classes, working together was difficult. Most of the time we had to work on the project after school hours to avoid conflict and confusion. The positioning of the website was also hard as we wanted our two of our features side by side, we eventually fixed this by using grids, columns, and changing the size of the timeline.
